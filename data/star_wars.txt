Star Wars is an American epic space opera franchise created by George Lucas.
The first Star Wars film was released in 1977 and became a worldwide pop-culture phenomenon.
The franchise has been expanded into various films and other media.
The Star Wars universe is set in a galaxy far, far away.
The main saga consists of three trilogies: the original trilogy, the prequel trilogy, and the sequel trilogy.
The original trilogy includes Episode IV: A New Hope (1977), Episode V: The Empire Strikes Back (1980), and Episode VI: Return of the Jedi (1983).
The prequel trilogy includes Episode I: The Phantom Menace (1999), Episode II: Attack of the Clones (2002), and Episode III: Revenge of the Sith (2005).
The sequel trilogy includes Episode VII: The Force Awakens (2015), Episode VIII: The Last Jedi (2017), and Episode IX: The Rise of Skywalker (2019).
The franchise also includes standalone films like Rogue One (2016) and Solo (2018).
The main characters include Luke Skywalker, Darth Vader, Princess Leia, Han Solo, and Obi-Wan Kenobi.
The Jedi are an ancient order of warriors who use the Force for good.
The Sith are the Jedi's ancient enemies who use the dark side of the Force.
The Force is an energy field created by all living things that binds the galaxy together.
Lightsabers are the signature weapons of the Jedi and Sith.
The Death Star is a massive space station capable of destroying entire planets.
The Millennium Falcon is Han Solo's iconic starship.
Tatooine is the desert planet where Luke Skywalker grew up.
Coruscant is the capital planet of the Galactic Republic.

The Clone Wars were a major conflict between the Galactic Republic and the Confederacy of Independent Systems.
The Mandalorians are a warrior culture known for their distinctive armor.
The Wookiees are a species of tall, hairy humanoids from the planet Kashyyyk.
The Ewoks are a species of small, furry humanoids from the forest moon of Endor.
The droids R2-D2 and C-3PO are among the most iconic characters in the franchise.
The cantina scene in A New Hope introduced various alien species to the Star Wars universe.
The phrase "I am your father" from The Empire Strikes Back is one of the most famous movie quotes.
The Star Wars theme music was composed by John Williams.
The franchise has inspired countless books, comics, video games, and other media.
